How it works

Pressup is a Mac Menubar app that you connect to your WordPress sites. When there are updates for your sites, Pressup lets you know. Update Core, Themes, Plugins and Translations individually, or just "Update Everything".

Pressup uses the WordPress REST API to securely request update data from your connected site.
